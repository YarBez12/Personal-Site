Danya Kashin

Age 28 years old
Place of birtd Kazan, Russia
Height 196
Marial status not married

Danila Kashin, better known as D.K. Inc, is one of the most recognizable video bloggers. Author of popular channels on YouTube, rap artist, singer and streamer. A significant part of Internet users know who Kashin is, or remembers a couple of lines from his songs.

Danila's biography began in Kazan in 1996, on November 6. It's hard to find information about Kashin's parents. In addition, judging by the sources, the data differs from each other. It is known that the father is not alive, and the relationship with the mother is not easy.

Since childhood, Danila was fond of music and dreamed of devoting his whole life to it. As a teenager, Kashin tried to turn his hobby into an art that everyone should know about. With the help of an accessible program, the future blogger created tracks and rapped with friends on the street. In 2011, he became interested in "Youtube".

Since 2016, DK has been a performer of his own tracks. Clips shot together with other bloggers appeared. Often the songs were based on a playing on words and strange plots. Subscribers, for example, were delighted with the song "Doshirak". According to the authors, instant noodles are a prohibited substance that can only be consumed underground. In 2017, DK and Crazy Mega Hell released the clip "Memes". A few months later, the number of views exceeded two million. 2018 was also eventful. In February, the parody clip "B pisses off" was released, the popularity of which was not yon the original from Marie Senn.

On March 9, Danya presented a video for a track called "Love". It is known that this DK text was inspired by the relationship between rapper Faith and blogger Mariana Ro. As part of the "Rap Akapelno" project, Kashin sang Mariana's song "Vzhuh-vzhuh". The remake gained an incredible number of views and deserved Roe's attention. The blogger appreciated the scale of Danila's popularity and left a positive comment under the video. Further, in April 2018, another track appeared - "The Hypocrite". In 2020, the blogger decided to diversify the content a little and moved the broadcasts to Twitch. However, this move was not very popular among Dani's fans and did not last long. Later, Kashin returned to his former work on YouTube. In parallel with blogging, the young man continued his musical activities, introducing subscribers to the author's novelties in social networks.

In the summer of the same year, Danila presented to the public a clip for the track "Everything is fine", and in November he pleased the audience with another video - now for the song "Under the Moon". In the Instagram account on the occasion of the release of the composition, the blogger wrote a post in which he said that he was initially preparing an EP, planning to include 3-4 tracks in it, and some of them even managed to record. However, he soon realized that "everything revolves around this demo", and decided to release "Under the Moon" as a single. The musical composition continued the following year - the track Ping Pong was released.

In 2022, under the new pseudonym Lil the Neel, Dania Kashin began making rap music, but at the moment he is not continuing this project. That same year, he returned to Twitch. This time, he began to have success and by 2024, DK had gained a huge audience on this platform.

In 2024, DK released 2 hits - "Don't Plant Love" and "Night", which sank into the hearts of viewers.

Most famous songs
Hypocrite
Released in 2017
A bright and provocative track in which Dania Kashin exposes the toxicity of a fake society, hypocrisy and double standards. With a powerful delivery and sharp lyrics, he demonstrates the gap between words and actions, raising themes of trust, sincerity and self-deception.
The song is written for those who like to listen to the truth about themselves, but only with a beat.
I Watch Anime
Released in 2018
An ironic track in which Dania Kashin humorously plays on stereotypes about anime fans and their hobbies. The song is filled with sarcasm and cultural references, emphasizing the author's uniqueness and self-irony.
K-pop is gaining popularity, but not here. I decided to fix this situation and release such a happyhardcore song!
2k
Released in 2020
A melancholic track that conveys an atmosphere of senseless rebellion, despair and a thirst for freedom surrounded by parties and chaos. Through images of loneliness, burning principles and dancing on the edge, Dania Kashin paints a picture of a generation trying to find meaning in a world full of contradictions.
Remember, the most stylish thing is to dress beautifully and dance to your principles until they burn out.
Praxx
Released in 2021
An emotional and dark track in which Dania Kashin expresses his inner fears, struggle with himself and a sarcastic attitude towards the world around him. It combines deep meaning, aggressive delivery and multi-layered metaphors, leaving the listener room for interpretation.
In short, I decided that I needed to try myself in something new, otherwise I started writing complex music.
Don't Plant Love (ft.TheBrianMaps)
Released in 2024
The second collaboration after six years and the first joint track by Danila Kashin and Brian Maps, which is a debut for Maxim. Their first joint work was released in March 2018 on Brian's channel in the format of a parody of the famous TV show "Swindlers in the Networks".
It's an important job for me, not just in terms of views or something like that. As a producer.
Night
Released in 2024
Night is Dani Kashin's first solo single in 2024, which was accompanied by great anticipation from his fans. The first snippet of this track appeared in early 2022. The release of the song was delayed due to the artist being dissatisfied with the final result many times.
The track “Night” is probably my biggest problem with music.
