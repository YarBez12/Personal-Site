Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

The second film in the Harry Potter series, Harry returns to Hogwarts School, where he faces a mysterious threat: someone has opened the Chamber of Secrets, unleashing a monster that is attacking the students. Harry, Hermione, and Ron set out to solve the mystery of the room, which will lead them to a new magical challenge.

Scream

A cult horror thriller in which an unknown masked killer terrorizes a small town. Sidney Prescott becomes the killer's main target, and she must unravel his identity while saving herself and her friends. The film is full of unexpected twists and is one of the founders of the slasher genre.
